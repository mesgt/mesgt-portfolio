CrimeAde

Your local crime tracker around Denver, Colorado. Know your data. Be safe.

I was a part of a five-man developer team who worked on this project for about a week. This app lets users view crime data for each state from the FBI API on a google map, as well as local, location-based crime data for the Denver area (MySQL database). Additionally, users may enter their own data if they were a victim/witness of a crime. For security reasons, all user data is kept anonymous. Since our goal is to help users be safe, our app provides randomized safety tips on each reload, as well as local emergency numbers. Currently our local and user data side focuses on users in the Denver area.

cal-Oracle

Easy way to track your caloric intake and stay within your daily goal.

I was part of a four-man team to work on this project. This app was built for a calorie-conscious user who wants to track their daily caloric intake. The user's daily goal is displayed at the top of the page and as the user enters the quantity and type of food, the total of calories consumed that day is displayed at the bottom of the page. As the user approaches the daily goal, the box displaying total calories consumed changes from green to yellow to red when the user exceeds the goal. In addition, the user is presented with a healthy recipe for the food item they enter as an encouragement to continue eating healthy.
