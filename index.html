The Everything App

cal-Oracle

Easy way to track your caloric intake and stay within your daily goal.

INTRO: As part of a four man team, I primarily worked on the recipe third party APIs and assisted with troubleshooting.

REASONING: This app was built for a calorie-conscious user who wants to track their daily caloric intake. The user's daily goal is displayed at the top of the page and as the user enters the quantity and type of food, the total of calories consumed that day is displayed at the bottom of the page. As the user approaches the daily goal, the box displaying total calories consumed changes from green to yellow to red when the user exceeds the goal.

TOOLS: API, Materialize CSS Framework, Modals
